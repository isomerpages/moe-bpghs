<h4>Netball – Team Spirit, Dedication, and Passion on the Court</h4>

<p></p>
<p>The Netball team has continued to show outstanding teamwork, determination,
and enthusiasm in every match and training session. Each player brought
energy and commitment to the court, supporting one another through challenges
and celebrating every success together.</p>
<p>Under the guidance of their coach, the team worked hard to develop their
skills, strengthen communication, and grow in confidence. Their efforts
were rewarded with strong performances, exciting games, and plenty of memorable
moments that highlighted their growth both as athletes and as teammates.</p>
<p>Beyond the competition, this team has built lasting friendships and shown
what true school spirit looks like — encouraging each other, having fun,
and representing our school with pride. This passion, perseverance, and
positivity continue to inspire the entire school community!</p>
