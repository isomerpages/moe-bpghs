<summary>About Us</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p>The Infocomm Club comprises artistic individuals and talents who are passionate
about producing works of digital arts, specifically in the realm of photography
and videography.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The club aims to foster a deeper appreciation for the arts while cultivating
important attributes such as team spirit, responsibility, initiative and
leadership within each of its members. Members meet on a weekly basis to
hone their skills in filming, 3D animation and photography.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Club also provides photography coverage for numerous school events
to capture precious moments that encapsulate the Bukit Panjang school spirit.</p>
</div>
