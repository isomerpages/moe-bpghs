<h4>(I) Eligibility Criteria </h4>
<p>Applicants must meet the following criteria under the respective talent
area:</p>
<p><strong>1. Basketball (Boy), Floorball (Boy), Netball (Girl), Volleyball (Boy &amp; Girl)</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Member of Basketball, Floorball, Netball or Volleyball CCA for at least
2 years preferred.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Student of Junior Sports Academy (JSA) programme preferred.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Relevant training and game experiences preferred.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Top 8 placing at National School Games (NSG) competitions preferred</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong>2. Concert Band (Boy/Girl)</strong>
</p>
<p>Meet at least one of the following:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Member of Band for at least 2 years.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Minimum of 2 years playing experience in woodwind, brass or percussion
instrument.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Outstanding achievements at national or international levels in Band.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong>3. Chinese Orchestra (Boy/Girl)</strong>
</p>
<p>Meet at least one of the following:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Demonstrate strong talent and interest in Chinese Orchestra.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Minimum of 2 years playing experience in Chinese Orchestra.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Outstanding achievements at national or international levels in Chinese
Orchestra.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><strong>4. Malay Language </strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Achieved consistently good academic results in Malay Language.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Active participation in activities related to the use of Malay Language.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h4>(II) Selection Process</h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Selection will be based on how well the applicant meets the eligibility
criteria. Proofs of applicant’s areas of strength and achievements are
essential. Meeting all the criteria does not guarantee that the student
will be shortlisted/given an offer.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shortlisted applicants will be required to participate in a selection
trial (for Sports and Games) / audition (for Band and Chinese Orchestra)
/ assessment (for Malay Language) and an interview.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>2.1 Sports DSA:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>General motor ability</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sports specific skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Applicants are to report in sports attire and sport shoes.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>2.2 Concert Band:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Sight reading: To be given by Band Director</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>2 Contrasting Pieces: To prepare 2 contrasting pieces of own choice</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Scales: Bb Concert; Eb Concert; Ab Concert; Db Concert; C Concert</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Applicants can choose to use their personal instrument or the one provided
by the school during the DSA audition.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>2.3 Chinese Orchestra:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Perform 2 contrasting pieces of choice on the instrument.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>May play selected excerpt of each piece.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Total time should not exceed 5 minutes</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Applicants can choose to use their personal instrument or the one provided
by the school during the DSA audition.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>2.4 Malay Language: Applicants are required to complete a written assessment.</p>

<h4>(IV) Application Procedure (7 May 2024, 11am – 3 Jun 2024, 3pm)</h4>
<p>Application for DSA-Sec can be submitted through the online DSA-Sec Portal.
The application is free-of-charge and will be open from 7 May 2024, 11am
to 3 Jun 2024, 3pm. For more details, please refer to MOE website – <a href="http://www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec</a>.</p>
<p>Parents and students are encouraged to choose schools wisely based on
the student’s aptitudes and strengths, bearing in mind the schools’ academic
and non-academic requirements, and the programmes available to develop
the area of talent.</p>
<p>Students who are successfully admitted to the school via DSA are expected
to honour their commitment to the school and participate in the activities
related to the talent they are selected for from year 1-4.</p>
